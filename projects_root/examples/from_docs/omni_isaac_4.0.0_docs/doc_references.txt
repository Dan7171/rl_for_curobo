######################## Vocabulary #########################
1. "Articulated" := having two or more sections connected by a flexible joint. Articulated robots are defined as robots containing rotary joints
Articulated robots are robotic arms with multiple joints (or rotary axes), similar to a human arm. These robots are highly flexible and used for precision tasks in manufacturing, assembly, welding, and even surgery.
